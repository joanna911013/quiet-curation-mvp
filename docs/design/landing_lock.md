## Landing IA (6 Sections)
- Hero — Purpose: state the promise and invite a 3‑minute screen‑loop break. Key elements: H1, sub, microcopy, primary CTA, secondary link, screen‑loop line.
- How — Purpose: explain the 3‑minute flow in plain steps. Key elements: 3 steps, time commitment note.

## MKT Locks
Target persona:
- Overstimulated knowledge workers who start and end their day on their phone, and want a small, quiet ritual without a feed.

Promise statement:
- A 3‑minute morning reset by email—one pairing, no feed, no noise.

For / Not for:
- For: people who want a small daily ritual; people who prefer email over apps; people who want calm context, not more content.
- Not for: people who want constant updates; people who want social feeds; people who want long, in‑depth essays.

Conversion‑critical FAQs (5–6):
- Is this a newsletter? / It’s one daily pairing, not a stream of updates.
- How long does it take? / About three minutes end‑to‑end.
- When do you send it? / Every morning at 09:00 KST.
- Can I stop anytime? / Yes, unsubscribe in one click.
- What will I receive? / One short literature excerpt + one verse + a brief connection.
- Do you track me? / No tracking beyond basic email delivery.

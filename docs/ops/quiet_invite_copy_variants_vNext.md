# Quiet Invite Copy Variants — vNext

## A) Subject Lines (EN/KR)

| intent | EN | KR |
| --- | --- | --- |
| minimal | Today’s reading | 오늘의 읽기 |
| warm | A quiet line for today | 오늘의 조용한 한 줄 |
| poetic | A small light, today | 오늘의 작은 빛 |
| direct | Today’s pairing | 오늘의 조합 |
| curiosity | One line, one verse | 한 줄, 한 절 |

## B) Email Body Variants (EN + KR)

Notes:
- Pairing snippet (if present) is max 2 lines, no labels, each line <= 90 chars.
- Word count target assumes the excerpt is 2–3 sentences (~40–60 words).
- If pairing is missing, omit the pairing snippet entirely.

### Variant 1 — Minimal / Warm

{literature_excerpt (2–3 sentences)}

{CTA_BUTTON}
{UNSUBSCRIBE_LINE}

## C) Microcopy Keys (EN/KR)

### CTA Button Text (3 options)

| key | EN | KR | notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| email.cta.open_today | Open today’s reading | 오늘의 읽기 열기 | Gentle primary CTA. |
| email.cta.continue | Continue | 계속하기 | Short and minimal. |
| email.cta.open | Open | 열기 | Shortest option. |

### Footer / Unsubscribe Line (2 options)

| key | EN | KR | notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| email.footer.unsubscribe | Unsubscribe | 구독 해지 | Plain, minimal. |
| email.footer.preferences | Manage email preferences | 이메일 설정 변경 | Softer alternative. |
